Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions.

What is roadmap.sh?

Roadmap.sh is a community effort to create roadmaps, guides and resources to help developers learn and grow. The goal is to help you find your way through the vast world of development, by providing you with a clear path and resources to follow.

How can I contribute?

You can contribute by creating a pull request on the GitHub repository with your changes. You can also help by sharing the project with your friends and colleagues.

Who is behind roadmap.sh?

Roadmap.sh is a community effort, and it is maintained by a group of volunteers. You can find the list of contributors on the GitHub repository.

How can I support roadmap.sh?

You can support roadmap.sh by contributing to the project, sharing it with your friends and colleagues, or by making a donation to help us cover the costs of hosting and maintaining the website.
